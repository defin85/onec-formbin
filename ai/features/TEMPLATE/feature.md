# Feature Contract

## Goal

Describe the feature in one paragraph.

## User-visible behavior

Describe what should change from the user's point of view.

## Interfaces involved

List the modules, commands, APIs, or screens affected by the change.

## Acceptance criteria

List the concrete outcomes that must be true when the feature is complete.

## Non-goals

List what this change must not expand into.

## Known risks

List the likely failure modes or regressions to watch during iteration.
